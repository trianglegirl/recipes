Noodles

Thai Butternut Squash Noodles

  • 1/2 onion
  • Clove of garlic
  • 1/2 butternut squash
  • Lemon grass
  • Couple kaffir lime leaves
  • Chilli
  • 300ml water ( more if cream coconut)
  • 1/2 tin coconut milk/ 50g cream coconut
  • Small carrot
  • Peppers ( red/orange)
  • Juice and zest of lime

Cook onion and garlic Add everything else except lime juice Serve with lime juice and noodle.
